Seletech FIELD LEVEL HAZARD ASSESSMENT

As a group:
STOP: Step back and observe.
THINK: Identify and review all tasks involved in the work scope.
ASSESS: Hazards, pathways, impact related to scope of work and site. Identify the hazards and controls you will use.
PLAN: Identify needed controls, verify implementation and document.
DISCUSS: The plan with all involved and complete the task.
REVISE: Repeat assessment with the group if conditions or tasks change.
